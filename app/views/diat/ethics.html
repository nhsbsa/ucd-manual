Ethics induction

Format: Workshop

Length: 1 day

Anyone involved in user research in the NHS Business Services Authority, Digital, Insight and Technology (DIaT) must go on this ethics training.

What you’ll learn

The session includes:

  • the Market Research Society’s Code of Conduct, and data protection laws and regulation
  • how we conduct research with various user groups
  • how we use data in research, including sharing, storing and handling user research data
  • ethical research
  • gaining voluntary informed consent and accessible consent forms

Who can attend

This is a mandatory session for user researchers in NHSBSA Digital, Insight and Technology (DIaT). It’s also open to other staff involved in research inside and outside of DIaT.

Related content

You may also find the following content useful:

  • user research on the GOV.UK Service Manual
  • training and events at the Market Research Society
